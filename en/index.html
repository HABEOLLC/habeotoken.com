About Habeo

HABEO is a Binance Smart Chain token (BEP-20) like no other.
Built from the ground up, it implements several proven concepts as well as some very innovative features that make it the most interesting token on the market:

  • Reflections.
  • Auto LP.
  • Auto burning.
  • Layered taxation with whale deterrants.
  • Throttled swaps (1 every 8 hours).
  • Self-hosted early distribution
  • Low taxes for acquiring and for transfering between wallets.
  • Transaction size limit.
  • A burn wallet that, when the burn is complete, will automatically increase reflections for all users.
  • Ownership fully renounced.
  • LP locked forever.

HABEO's goal is to make transactions cheap, easy and fair for everyone.

Frequently Asked Questions

These are questions that have been asked a few times so we think that providing this information in advance will help you learn about HABEO.

  • How do I acquire HABEO?

    There are two ways to obtain HABEO at this moment. You can get it during the early distribution period by simply sending BNB from your wallet to the contract address (read about the accepted quantities and corresponding number of HABEO tokens in the whitepaper) OR you can swap for it in PancakeSwap once the early distribution is closed. There will be other ways to obtain HABEO when we onboard other exchanges. We'll update you all with the information as soon as it is available.

  • Is the ownership of the contract renounced?

    Short answer: Yes. Long answer: Yes, you can look at the code. Line #672 is the call to the function that sets the ownership of HABEO to the 0x address.

  • Is the liquidity locked?

    Yes, the initial liquidity is locked forever. You can take a look at line #1168 in the code. The addLiquidity function needs a wallet address to return the LP tokens to. We've provided the wallet with address 0 to that request. This is the burn wallet so the LP tokens are effectively burnt. This way, they are never to be recovered. Without access to the LP tokens, liquidity can never be removed.

  • What happens if I send BNB to the contract address AFTER the early distribution is done?

    If this happens, the BNB will be forwarded to the dev wallet as a donation. Once the early distribution period is done, HABEO can no longer be acquired by sending BNB to the contract address.

  • How do I learn more about HABEO?

    You can learn more about HABEO and crypto in general by taking a look at our documents and videos. They are available in multiple languages.
